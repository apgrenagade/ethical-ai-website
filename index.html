About Us

We strive to promote responsible AI use in education by developing ethical policies and ensuring that AI enhances learning without compromising academic integrity.

Our Founder

Yuvan Kothari is the founder of Ethical AI in Schools. With a background in computer science education and leadership, Yuvan has worked extensively in developing AI curricula and promoting responsible AI practices. His experience with ByteBuildersAI, tutoring, and leadership roles in high school has fueled his passion for ensuring AI is used ethically in education. His vision for this organization is to empower students and educators to integrate AI responsibly while maintaining academic integrity.
